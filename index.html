Why Minky

Silver technology

A cloth that doesn't harbour bacteria

Silver is woven through the microfibre and stops bacteria growing on the cloth. Because it's part of the fibre, the protection lasts beyond 50 washes.

Grid-weave microfibre

Lifts grease, smears and fingerprints

A high-density grid weave puts more fibre against the surface with every pass. It dries fast and keeps its performance wash after wash.
